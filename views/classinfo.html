Classes


Admissions open for First year in the Advanced Diploma in Carnatic Music (Vocal).



Duration:

3 years with 2 semesters every year, Mid July – November end and Mid January – June end.


Classes:

Monday thru Friday 0800 am – 12:30 pm


Eligibility:

Applicants should have minimum educational qualification of +2 (successfully completed). Age should be between 18 to 25 years. They should be able to sing Varnams, Kritis and have a reasonable level of Manodharma. Applicants should send a hard copy of their bio – data giving full details of their musical training to the following address in a sealed envelope clearly marked “Admission to ASCM”. The Academic Coordinator The Music Academy Madras, 168/306, TTK Road, Chennai 600014 Shortlisted Applicants will be called for an interview by end June and selected candidates will be intimated by email.
